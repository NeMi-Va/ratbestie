Rat Nutrition

Balanced Diet:

Use a special premade mix or rat pellets bought from a store.
You can also make your own rat food mix:
Making the rat food mix
If you notice that your rats are being picky and not eating a certain part of the mix switch to pellets.
Try to use multiple types of pellets from different companies as it warrants that they get an appropriate number of different types of nutrients.

Feeding:

Feed your rats 1-2 times a day.
An adult rat needs around 60 calories/day.
Amount of food following your used food brand recommendation or generally 15-20 grams/average adult rat/day. So, if you are feeding your rat twice a day that would mean 7-10 grams/feeding/rat.

Special Diets:

Consult a veterinarian for rats with specific dietary needs such as allergies, obesity, or other health conditions.

Hydration:

Ensure fresh clean water is available at all times!
